A Letter from Elsa

Hi there friends!!

I recently felt that I wanted to take steps towards a more sustainable lifestyle but didn't have the tools or resources easily available to do so. I made this document to try and make sustainability a little more tangible and accessible in our daily lives. It also serves as a reminder that our choices have the power to create a meaningful impact. Before you dive into it, I hope that you take a couple things with you.

First, YOU, yes YOU are extraordinary in your power—don't waste it away! Empower yourself by learning. When moving through life, let constant curiosity criticize your own and others behavior and always seek answers with an open mind. We do have a choice about what we buy, and often it takes one search to find an environmentally friendly product or solution. Stay curious!

Second, become competitive. Approach sustainability as is if it is a game that you are dying to win and can't bear to lose. Let competitiveness add a level of urgency so your actions become significant.

Lastly, I am not asking you to take everything on this list and apply it to your life. Only that you aim to get 1%-5% more aware and thoughtful each day. The incremental changes will build up, so start small. Swap your toilet paper, begin examining your kitchen, or start becoming heads up what your local community has to offer. There are indeed lots of ways for us to make a difference. Sometimes it requires us to be proactive and sacrifice the convenience of clicking on Amazon.

This document is just starting so please please please, email me or text me if you find any amazing organizations, companies, products that are sustainable, environmentally friendly, and are actively working to stop climate change! AND pass it on to others!

It starts with you.

Elsa
